Once you have collected your sample, press "Develop Sample" button below. You will be given 1 minute to leave the swab with your sample in the extraction tube.

Then, 1 minute to withdraw the swab from the extraction tube, push the nozzle cap tightly on the extraction tube to avoid any leaks and to gently squeeze 4 drops of liquid onto the sample well (marked S) on the test cassette (press "Transfer Sample").

Once ready, press "Get Test Results" for 15 minutes countdown to your results.
